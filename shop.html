SHOP THE SNA HANDBOOK

Welcome to the SNA Schedule & Wellness Handbook, a must-have resource designed to support SNA's in their vital role within schools. This comprehensive handbook provides practical tools, wellness guidance, and essential resources to help SNAs manage their daily responsibilities with greater ease, focus, and well-being.

What's Inside?

The SNA Schedule & Wellness Handbook offers a blend of tools and resources tailored to the unique needs of SNAs, with a focus on both professional efficiency and personal well-being.

Key Features

  • School Year Schedule: Plan your year with ease, including monthly overview, monthly review & daily pages for your own recording.
  • Wellness & Self-Care Section Learn strategies for maintaining mental and physical well-being in the challenging but rewarding role of an SNA.
  • Templates & Record Sheets: Easily track student progress, attendance, and key information with ready-to-use templates and all free to download.
  • Resources for SNAs: Access links and helpful information all set out by the dept. of Education.

Why Choose the SNA Schedule & Wellness Handbook?

The SNA Schedule & Wellness Handbook is designed with the understanding that SNAs not only support students but also play a crucial role in creating a positive and inclusive school environment. The handbook provides:

  • Practical solutions for everyday challenges faced in the classroom.
  • Wellness-focused content to ensure SNAs maintain balance and avoid burnout.
  • Time-saving tools that streamline administrative tasks, allowing SNAs to focus on what matters most—supporting students.

Who Is It For?

This handbook is ideal for SNA's working in primary, secondary, and special education settings. It’s also a great resource for schools looking to support their SNA teams with valuable tools and well-being practices.

New for 2025-2026

The second edition of the handbook for the 2025-2026 academic year features enhanced sections, including new resources based on feedback from SNAs across Ireland. With an updated format, additional wellness tips, and improved record-keeping templates, this edition ensures that SNAs are better equipped to navigate their roles with confidence and care.
